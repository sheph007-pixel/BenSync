Keep it consistent

Usage Rules

The logo is the wordmark only. Never combine it with the monogram or the sync mark into a lockup.
Never put the color wordmark on Dark Navy — the navy “Ben” disappears. Use the reversed version instead.
Use the navy/white 1-color versions for single-color print, embroidery, and engraving — and send vendors the SVG files.
Keep the monogram to tight spaces — favicon, app icon, social avatar — and nothing else.
Give the logo room. Keep clear space around it of at least the height of the “B”.
Don't alter the wordmark. No stretching, recoloring, effects, or resetting it in another font.
