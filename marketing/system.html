The problem

You know this workload too well.

Serving a group the traditional way means assembling and babysitting every piece yourself. The work lands on you, and none of it is why your client hired you.

Quoting carrier by carrier Spreadsheeting medical, dental, vision, and supplemental one at a time.
Stitching vendors together Administrator, pharmacy, enrollment, app, payment card, COBRA, all separate.
Chasing enrollment Eligibility files, feeds, and follow-ups across every system.
Service landing on you Bills, claims questions, and renewals coming back to your desk.
What we did about it

We built the whole stack once, so you never build it again.

BenSync assembled the pieces into one program: a level-funded health plan at the center, established carriers and administrators around it, with enrollment, billing, member support, and renewals already connected. It has served employer groups since 2013 and it runs every day.
