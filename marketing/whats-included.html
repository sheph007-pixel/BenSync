What's included

Everything an employer needs in one benefits program.

BenSync is a bundled system, not just a health plan. Every component below is assembled, integrated, and delivered as one coordinated program, with one bill.

01
Health Plan A medical plan designed to lower costs through smarter funding and clear pricing: administered by HealthEZ, with SRS managing the captive and Davies as program actuary.
02
Pharmacy Prescription drug coverage with clear pricing and no hidden markups, managed by MedOne.
03
Dental & Vision Dental coverage through Guardian, fully insured or level funded, and vision coverage through VSP, built into the bundled program.
04
Supplemental Benefits Life, disability, accident, critical illness, hospital coverage, and other options through carriers including Guardian.
05
Online Enrollment Online enrollment through Employee Navigator, with call center support when needed.
06
Member App & Navigation HealthJoy app and support resources to help members navigate care and understand their benefits.
07
Payment Card Paytient card to help employees manage out-of-pocket healthcare costs.
08
Consolidated Billing One coordinated bill for the bundled program.
09
COBRA & Administration Help with required paperwork and administration when employees leave or change coverage, administered by Flores.
10
Renewals & Service Ongoing support, reporting, and renewal coordination, year after year.
This would be very hard to assemble yourself. BenSync already did it: evaluated, coordinated, and packaged into one ready-to-use program with one bill and one service model.
