Why we built this

BenSync exists to serve insurance professionals.

Employers trust their advisors, and advisors deserve a wholesale partner that carries the operational load behind them. Our commitment is to stay current as the industry changes and to put great people on the problems our advisors bring us. That is why we built this program: so you can deliver the benefits your employer clients and their employees need, without building any of it yourself.

How the program is funded

The BenSync health plan is level funded and self funded, built on reference-based pricing for clear, fair claim costs. Employers get predictable monthly payments with the upside of self funding. [CONFIRM: The program is backed by a captive arrangement managed by SRS, the largest independent captive management firm, which has supported the program for years.] Dental, vision, and supplemental benefits are fully insured through established carriers. Brokers get a complete story: a transparent, level-funded health plan strategy on the medical side, and familiar fully insured coverage everywhere else.
