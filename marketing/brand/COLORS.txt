Hex/RGB are the definitive digital values. CMYK values are standard
uncalibrated conversions for four-color process print; confirm proofs
with your printer for critical color matching.

Headings + logo: Sora, weight 600-700 (logo is Sora 600)
Body text + buttons: Manrope, regular

LOGO = wordmark only: "Ben" Navy + "Sync" Green (White + Mint on dark).
ICONS (favicon, app icon, avatar): "B" monogram, navy top / green bottom.
Sync-arrows mark (icon-*.svg / icon-*.png) is a secondary graphic element.

FILE FORMATS
svg/  vector masters (wordmark, monogram, sync mark) - use for print,
      signage, embroidery digitizing, engraving, and any large format.
      Infinitely scalable, colors embedded.
png/  transparent raster exports for screens and documents. Wordmark is
      4064px wide (13.5 in at 300 DPI); monogram and marks are 1024px.
      app-icon.png and avatar-circle.png are opaque by design.
