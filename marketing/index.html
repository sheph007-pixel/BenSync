Common questions

Frequently asked questions.

What is BenSync?

BenSync is a wholesale employee benefits program built for insurance advisors. It bundles a level funded health plan, pharmacy, dental, vision, and supplemental coverage with enrollment, billing, member support, and renewals into one program with one bill.

Who can offer BenSync?

BenSync is distributed only through select broker partners and is never sold direct. Brokers deliver the program under their own brand and keep the client relationship.

What size employer is a good fit?

BenSync is built for employers with 5 to 250 enrolled employees, and it is available nationwide.

When can a group join, and when does it renew?

Groups can join any month of the year, with no need to wait for a set date. The master program runs on a calendar year, so every group shares a common renewal.

How is the health plan funded?

The health plan is level funded, a type of self funding, built on reference-based pricing. It is backed by a captive program that has operated since 2013, managed by SRS, Strategic Risk Solutions, the largest independent captive manager in the world.

Can employees keep their doctors?

Yes. The plan is not a narrow network. Members can see any provider, with contracted rates from over 1 million providers nationwide, and hospital care priced by clear reference-based pricing.

What oversight stands behind the program?

The program's captives are licensed and regulated by the State of Vermont and the State of Alabama and audited by independent CPA firm Larson Gross. The captive program won the SRS Captivator Award.

How does an employer get BenSync?

Through their insurance broker or consultant. Employers ask their broker about BenSync, and brokers can get started through the contact form on this site.
