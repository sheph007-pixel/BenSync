What BenSync is

The program layer for employee benefits.

BenSync is the program layer that connects best-in-class partners into one bundled employee benefits program: a level-funded health plan built on transparent, reference-based pricing at the center, with dental, vision, and supplemental coverage from established carriers around it. A good fit is any group with 5 or more enrolled employees.

We built it in a wholesale capacity for insurance professionals, and it has served employer groups since 2013. You deliver the benefits your clients need; the assembly is already done.
