What BenSync is

The program layer for small-group benefits.

BenSync is not just a health plan. Not just a claims administrator. Not just enrollment technology. It's the program layer that connects best-in-class partners into one bundled small-group benefits solution.

Small employers can't realistically assemble the right vendors, systems, enrollment, billing, and support on their own, and most agencies don't want to build every piece themselves. BenSync already did the assembly, so brokers and employers get the finished program.
